About Me

Born and raised in the beautiful Principality of Andorra nestled in the Pyrenees, I've always been drawn to bridging different worlds – whether it's the cultural divide between mountain traditions and modern innovation, or the technical gap between human needs and technological solutions.

My journey began with Industrial Engineering studies at IQS School of Engineering in Barcelona, where I discovered my passion for solving complex problems through systematic thinking and technological innovation. But it was the pursuit of my MBA at GLOBIS University in Tokyo, under the prestigious Monbukagakusho scholarship, that truly opened my eyes to the transformative power of cross-cultural collaboration.

I believe that the future of industry lies not just in advanced technology, but in how we humanize that technology. At Yokogawa Electric Corporation, I'm currently leading the charge in digital transformation strategies that put people at the center of smart manufacturing. My work focuses on developing robotics and drone solutions that don't replace human intelligence, but amplify it.

As a patent holder for autonomous patrol systems, I'm passionate about creating technologies that enable mission planning and execution while maintaining the human element that drives true innovation. My multicultural perspective – speaking Catalan, Spanish, English, and Japanese – allows me to see challenges from multiple angles and build bridges between Eastern and Western business cultures.

My mission is simple: to revolutionize industrial operations through AI, Digital Twin, and IoT technologies that enhance both safety and human potential, creating a future where technology serves humanity's greatest aspirations.
